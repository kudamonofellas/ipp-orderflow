IPP OrderFlow — app assets
==========================

To use your real IPP logo across the app (login screen + header):

  Save the logo image here as:   logo.png

Recommended: the WHITE version (white "iP" + "PT INTI PANGAN PERKASA"),
since the app's header/sidebar are dark. A transparent PNG works best.

Once logo.png is in this folder, refresh the app and it appears automatically.
Until then, a simple red "iP" placeholder (logo.svg) is shown.
